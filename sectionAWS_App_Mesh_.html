Understanding AWS App Mesh

AWS App Mesh acts as a central control plane that enables organizations to connect, monitor, and secure microservices running on AWS. It simplifies the complexity of microservices communication by providing features like service discovery, load balancing, and traffic management. App Mesh facilitates communication between services across different compute environments, such as Amazon ECS, Amazon EKS, and Amazon EC2 instances, while allowing developers to focus on building individual services without concerning themselves with the intricacies of networking. Scholarly articles [8] often emphasize the significance of service mesh solutions like AWS App Mesh in modern microservices-based architectures. Researchers highlight the benefits of centralized control and visibility in microservices communication, which contribute to improved reliability and observability.
